Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a popular dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Various toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the base.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and your choice of toppings.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
